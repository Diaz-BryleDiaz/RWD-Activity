You may now start your exercise (Inclined Push-Up)

  1. Find a sturdy surface: This could be a bench, a low wall, or even a sturdy chair. Make sure it’s stable enough to support your weight.
  2. Position your hands: Place your hands shoulder-width apart on the surface. Your palms should be flat, with fingers pointing forward.
  3. Get into a plank position: Extend your legs straight behind you, balancing on your toes. Your body should form a straight line from your head to your heels. Engage your core to keep your back flat.
  4. Lower your body: Bend your elbows and slowly lower your chest towards the surface. Keep your elbows slightly tucked in towards your body, rather than flaring out.
  5. Push back up: Once your chest is just above the surface, push through your hands to straighten your arms and return to the starting position. Make sure to keep your body in a straight line throughout the movement.
