You may now begin your exercise (Straight Bar Dips)

  1. Find a straight bar: This could be on a dip station, a pull-up bar, or any sturdy horizontal bar that can support your weight.
  2. Grip the bar: Place your hands on the bar slightly wider than shoulder-width apart. Your palms should be facing forward.
  3. Get into position: Lift yourself up so that your arms are straight and your body is hanging below the bar.
  4. Lower your body: Bend your elbows and lower your body by leaning slightly forward. Keep your core engaged and your legs straight or slightly bent behind you. Lower yourself until your shoulders are below your elbows.
  5. Push back up: Straighten your arms and push your body back up to the starting position. Ensure your movements are controlled and your form remains consistent.
