How the FitQuest Reward System Works

At FitQuest, we believe in celebrating your fitness journey and rewarding your progress! As you complete challenges and earn points, you'll unlock exciting rewards. Here's how it works:

Badge Rewards

For every milestone you reach, you'll unlock a special badge that reflects your dedication to improving your fitness. These rewards are designed to motivate you to keep pushing toward your fitness goals!

  • Bronze Badge: Earned after reaching 50 points. Congratulations on starting your fitness journey!
  • Silver Badge: Earned after reaching 100 points. You're making great progress – keep it up!
  • Gold Badge: Earned after reaching 200 points. You've achieved greatness in your fitness journey – amazing work!

When you reach these point thresholds, you'll be greeted with an alert that congratulates you and a message displaying your new badge!
