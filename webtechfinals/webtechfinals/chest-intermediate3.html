You may now begin your exercise (Box Dip)

  1. Find a sturdy box or bench. Sit on the edge of the box with your hands gripping the edge, fingers pointing forward.
  2. Extend your legs out in front of you, keeping your heels on the ground. Your arms should be straight and your body supported by your hands.
  3. Get into position: Lift yourself up so that your arms are straight and your body is hanging below the bar.
  4. Bend your elbows and slowly lower your body towards the ground. Keep your elbows pointing straight back, not out to the sides. Lower yourself until your elbows form a 90-degree angle.
  5. Press through your palms to straighten your arms and lift your body back to the starting position. Keep your movements controlled and maintain good form.
