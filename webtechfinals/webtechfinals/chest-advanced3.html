You may now begin your exercise (Explosive Push-ups)

  1. Start in a standard push-up position with your hands shoulder-width apart and your body in a straight line from head to heels.
  2. Lower your chest towards the ground by bending your elbows, keeping your core engaged and maintaining a straight body line.
  3. Explosively push through your hands to lift your body off the ground, allowing your hands to leave the floor.
  4. As you land, soften the impact by bending your elbows and immediately transition into the next repetition.
