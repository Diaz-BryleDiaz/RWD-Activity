You may now begin your exercise (Guillotine Bench Press)

  1. Set Up: Lie on a flat bench with your feet flat on the floor and your eyes directly under the barbell.
  2. Grip the Bar: Grab the barbell with a wider-than-shoulder-width grip. Your wrists should be straight, and your elbows should be at a natural angle.
  3. Lift Off: Take a deep breath and engage your core. Lift the bar off the rack by straightening your arms. Hold the bar directly above your neck.
  4. Lower the Bar: Slowly lower the barbell towards the base of your neck, just above your clavicles. Keep your elbows flared out to the sides. Control the descent to avoid bouncing the bar off your neck.
  5. Press Up: Push the barbell back up to the starting position by extending your arms. Exhale as you press the bar up. Keep the movement controlled and ensure your body remains stable.
  6. Re-rack: Once you’ve completed your set, carefully rack the barbell back onto the bench’s supports.
