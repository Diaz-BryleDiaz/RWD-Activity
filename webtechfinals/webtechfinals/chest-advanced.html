Barbell Hooklying Bench Press

The barbell hooklying bench press is a bench press variation performed with a barbell while keeping the knees bent and feet flat on the bench, enhancing core activation and stability while targeting the chest, shoulders, and triceps.(50pts after turn in)

Guillotine Bench Press

The **guillotine bench press** is a chest-focused exercise where the barbell is lowered to the neck instead of the chest, emphasizing the upper chest but requiring strict control to avoid injury.(45pts after turn in)

Explosive Push-ups

The explosive push-up is a dynamic bodyweight exercise where you push off the ground with enough force for your hands to leave the surface, building upper body power and strength. (45 pts after turn in)
